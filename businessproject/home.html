Book Cafe

Café du Ciel is the ultimate spot for book lovers and coffee addicts alike. Founded in 2018, Café du Ciel uses timeless blends and recipes, while being innovative. Whether for a simple coffee date or cramming for finals, Café du Ciel welcomes all who need a tranquil environment. We also offer a wide variety of books, so one might find a hidden gem.
